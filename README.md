# TrashTrack 🚛♻️
**Making waste disposal smarter with real-time alerts and location-based reminders.**

## 📝 Description
**TrashTrack** is a smart waste collection digital service designed to improve community waste management by notifying residents of the exact time and day for garbage pickup in their area.

## 📖 Overview
Developed at **Mindanao State University - Marawi City**, TrashTrack aims to create an efficient system that helps communities manage their trash on time and prevents overflow in their surroundings. Our mission is to reduce trash buildup and improve daily waste handling for households and local areas through a clear, feature-rich digital platform.

## ⚠️ The Problem
Improper waste disposal remains a critical issue because many households are uncertain about the appropriate time and manner to dispose of their garbage. This leads to:
*   **Environmental Impact:** Only about **40% of waste in Marawi City is properly collected**; the rest is often dumped in open areas.
*   **Public Health Risks:** Improper disposal causes unsanitary surroundings, foul odors, and increased risks to community health.
*   **Inefficiency:** Local government units face high management costs and frequent complaints due to inconsistent collection schedules.

## ✅ The Solution
TrashTrack provides a **Digital Service (Mobile App)** that bridges the gap between residents and waste collection services. By utilizing real-time tracking, the app ensures that households know exactly when to bring out their trash, reducing the time waste sits on the street.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
*   **Frontend:** React.js
*   **Database:** MySQL
*   **Location Services:** Google Maps API
*   **Design/Prototyping:** Figma and Canva

## ✨ Key Features
*   **Real-time Collection Schedules:** Notifications for the exact timing of garbage pickups.
*   **Location-based Alerts:** Real-time tracking of collection vehicles to keep residents informed of nearby activity.
*   **Waste Segregation Reminders:** Guidance on how to properly sort recyclables and non-recyclables.
*   **Community Updates:** Notifications regarding local waste management initiatives and schedule changes.
*   **User-Friendly Dashboard:** A clean, easy-to-navigate interface designed for household use.

## 👥 Target Audience
*   **Primary Segment:** Urban and rural residents who need to dispose of household waste correctly and maintain a hygienic living environment.
*   **Secondary Segment:** Local Government Units (LGUs) and municipal waste management offices responsible for community cleanliness.
